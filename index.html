05

What every owner asks first

Will it sound robotic? +
You've got a recording of it above. Listen and decide for yourself. Callers regularly finish the whole conversation without realising it wasn't a person, and it never gets flustered on the fourth call of the hour.
What if it gets a question it can't answer? +
It doesn't guess. Anything medical, unusual, or outside what you've approved gets a message taken or the call passed to your team, and you see a full transcript of every conversation either way.
Is this safe for my clients' information? +
The agent handles enquiries and bookings, not medical records, and it never gives medical advice. Conversation data is stored securely and belongs to you.
Does it replace my front desk? +
No. It takes the calls they physically can't: after hours, weekends, and the ones that ring while they're with a client. Your team keeps the in-person work that actually needs them.
What do I have to set up? +
Nothing. I build it around how your spa already works: your treatments, your prices, your calendar. You review and approve everything before it takes a single live call.
Does it work with my booking system? +
It books directly into your calendar and works alongside the systems you already run. We'll confirm the exact fit on the call, that's part of the point of it.
